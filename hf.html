刹车片组成

Composition of Brake Pads

刹车片一般由钢板、粘接隔热层和摩擦块组成。

Brake lining is generally composed of steel plate, adhesive heat insulation layer and friction block.

钢板应进行涂装以防止生锈。涂装工艺使用SMT-4炉温跟踪器检测涂装过程的温度分布，确保产品质量。

The steel plate should be painted to prevent rust. The coating process uses SMT-4 furnace temperature tracker to detect the temperature distribution of the coating process to ensure quality.

我们采用先进的生产工艺和严格的质量控制，确保每一片刹车片都符合高标准的安全要求。

We adopt advanced production processes and strict quality control to ensure that each brake pad meets high safety standards.

异常刹车噪音分析

Analysis of Abnormal Brake Noise

这种声音主要是刹车片与刹车盘摩擦产生的声音。

This kind of sound is mainly the sound of friction between brake pad and brake disc.

许多车主会发现，在洗车后或雨后刚踩刹车时，会有明显的异常噪音。

Many car owners will find that there is a noticeable abnormal noise when they step on the brakes just after washing the car or after rain.

我们的刹车片经过特殊设计和材料配比，有效降低异常噪音，提供更平稳的制动体验。

Our brake pads are specially designed and formulated to effectively reduce abnormal noise and provide a smoother braking experience.

何时更换刹车片

When to Replace Brake Pads

当刹车片磨损、老化或制动失效时，必须及时更换，否则可能导致制动距离变长、制动效果降低，甚至造成安全隐患。

When the brake pad is worn, aged or brake failure, it must be replaced in time, otherwise it may lead to longer braking distance, lower braking effect, and even cause potential safety hazards.

建议定期检查刹车片厚度（一般小于3mm需更换），并选择适合车型的正品配件，应由专业人员进行更换。

It is recommended to regularly check the thickness of the brake pad (generally less than 3mm to be replaced), and select the genuine parts suitable for the vehicle model, which should be replaced by professionals.

We provide a variety of high-quality brake pads to meet different vehicle models and driving needs.
