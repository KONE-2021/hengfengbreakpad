Composition of Brake Pads

Brake pads are generally composed of steel plate, adhesive heat insulation layer, and friction block.

The steel plate should be painted to prevent rust. The coating process uses SMT-4 furnace temperature tracker to detect the temperature distribution of the coating process to ensure quality.

The insulation layer is made of non-heat-conductive materials for heat insulation and noise reduction.

The friction block consists of friction materials and adhesives. When braking, it is pressed against the brake disc or brake drum to generate friction, thereby achieving vehicle deceleration and braking.

We adopt advanced production processes and strict quality control to ensure that each brake pad meets high safety standards.

Analysis of Abnormal Brake Noise

Abnormal brake noise is mainly the sound of friction between brake pad and brake disc.

Many car owners will find that there is noticeable abnormal noise when they step on the brakes just after washing the car or after rain. This is because the brake disc surface has rust or moisture, causing changes in friction coefficient.

Our brake pads are specially designed and formulated to effectively reduce abnormal noise and provide a smoother braking experience.

Solutions:

  • Lightly press the brake pedal several times during gentle driving to clear moisture and rust from the brake disc surface
  • Use high-quality brake pads, such as Hengfeng brand products
  • Regularly check the brake system to ensure correct installation

When to Replace Brake Pads

When the brake pad is worn, aged or brake failure occurs, it must be replaced in time, otherwise it may lead to longer braking distance, lower braking effect, and even cause potential safety hazards.

Replacement Criteria:

  • Brake pad thickness less than 3mm requires replacement
  • Abnormal noise or vibration occurs
  • Significant increase in braking distance
  • Abnormal brake pedal feel

It is recommended to regularly check the thickness of the brake pad, and select the genuine parts suitable for the vehicle model, which should be replaced by professionals.
